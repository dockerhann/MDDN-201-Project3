LOGIN to social media. SCAN your facial features. findYOURSELF.

The aim of FindYourself is to return to you power over your personal details, giving back your control over your online personality. FindYourself is an online search engine that scours the web for your digital data, then creates your unique personality profile based on this information. Your personality profile will be generated as a virtual environment, showing you where each piece of information came from, and how it altered your online presence. In this space, you will be able to edit and remove any information that you don't want made public. FindYourself puts the power back in your hands.
